MEET CYNDI

As a teacher, mother of three, and community leader, I decided to run for State Representative to give a voice to those who need fair representation in our government. With 41.3% of the state budget supporting public education in Kentucky, it is necessary to have a voice at the table of those who speak for the needs of our children. With a community in distress, we need a voice for our citizens experiencing addiction. With a threat to the dismantling of critical health care services, we need a voice for protecting Kentuckians. I grew up in Shelby County and after attending college at Eastern Kentucky University, I returned to the community I love to raise my family. I want to be your voice for Shelby County.
